ENGINEERED FEATURES

1) degree_category
- school / ug / pg / phd / others

2) red_flag (0 to 7)
Count of triggered risk conditions:
- academic_pressure >= 4
- financial_stress >= 4
- work_study_hours >= 10
- study_satisfaction <= 2
- sleep_duration = less than 5 hours
- dietary_habits = unhealthy
- suicidal_thoughts = yes

3) wellness
CASE
  WHEN red_flag <= 2 THEN 'high'
  WHEN red_flag <= 5 THEN 'moderate'
  ELSE 'low'
END

4) support_priority (triage layer)
- critical
- high priority
- moderate priority
- preventive high risk
- preventive watchlist
- stable

KEY REMINDER
This project is meant for screening and support prioritization. Predictions and dashboard outputs should be reviewed by humans and should not be treated as clinical diagnosis.
